Results & Expectations

Neil's students have averaged 100–150 point SAT improvements, 5–10 point ACT gains, and 70–100 point GRE/GMAT improvements. Results depend on starting score, time available, and effort between sessions. Neil will always set honest expectations upfront — the free consultation is a good time to discuss what's achievable for your specific situation.

Most students notice meaningful progress within 3–5 sessions as foundational gaps are identified and addressed. Significant score gains typically require 8–12 weeks of consistent work. Students who complete practice between sessions consistently progress faster than those who don't.
