Top Global Study Destinations

Key information for students considering world-class universities outside the traditional English-speaking destinations.

🇸🇬 Singapore

NUS & NTU

NUS Ranks #8 Globally in QS 2025 — Among Asia's Most Competitive

The National University of Singapore (NUS) and Nanyang Technological University (NTU) are consistently ranked among the top 15 universities in the world. Both accept GMAT/GRE for graduate programs. Undergraduate admission for international students is highly selective — apply early in the September–November window for August intake.

Scholarships

ASEAN Undergraduate Scholarship & NUS Merit Scholarships Available

NUS offers the ASEAN Undergraduate Scholarship, Science & Technology Undergraduate Scholarship, and various faculty-specific awards for top international students. Most scholarships cover full tuition, accommodation, and a living allowance. Apply alongside your undergraduate application.

MBA Singapore

NUS & NTU MBA Programs — Strong GMAT Scores Essential

NUS Business School's MBA is ranked among Asia's top 5. The average GMAT score for admitted students is around 660–680. NTU's Nanyang MBA is also highly regarded in the region. Both programs offer strong finance, tech, and Asia-Pacific business networks.

🇭🇰 Hong Kong

HKU & HKUST

University of Hong Kong & HKUST Among Asia's Top 10 Universities

HKU (ranked ~17 globally in QS 2025) and HKUST are among Asia's most prestigious institutions. Both offer English-taught programs at undergraduate and graduate levels. International undergraduate applications typically open October–November for September admission.

2025–2026

Read more

Graduate Programs

Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme — Up to HK$331,200/year

The Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme (HKPFS) offers one of Asia's most generous doctoral fellowships: HK$331,200/year stipend plus a HK$13,500 travel allowance. Awards are made to outstanding students regardless of country of origin or academic discipline.

MBA Hong Kong

HKUST & HKU MBA Programs Accept GMAT and GRE Scores

HKUST Business School's full-time MBA is ranked among Asia's top 3 by the Financial Times. The average GMAT for admitted students is ~680. HKU's MBA focuses on China business and is popular with students targeting Asian careers in finance and consulting.

🇯🇵 Japan

Top Universities

University of Tokyo & Kyoto University — Japan's Most Prestigious Institutions

The University of Tokyo (UTokyo) ranks ~28 globally in QS 2025, and Kyoto University ranks in the top 50. Both offer English-taught graduate programs. UTokyo's PEAK undergraduate program accepts international students directly — apply November–December for September intake.

2025–2026

Read more

MEXT Scholarship

Japanese Government MEXT Scholarship — Full Funding for International Students

The MEXT (Monbukagakusho) Scholarship covers full tuition, monthly stipend (~¥143,000), and return airfare for undergraduate and graduate study in Japan. Apply through Japanese embassies in April–June for the following academic year. Competition is high — strong academic records are essential.

2025–2026

Read more

English Programs

Growing Number of English-Taught Programs in Japan

Japanese universities are rapidly expanding English-taught programs to attract international talent. Waseda, Keio, Osaka, and Tohoku universities all offer strong English-medium graduate programs in STEM, business, and social sciences. The EJU exam is required for most undergraduate applicants.

🇦🇪 Middle East

UAE

NYU Abu Dhabi & AUS — World-Class Education With Full Scholarships

NYU Abu Dhabi offers one of the world's most generous financial aid packages — full scholarships covering tuition, housing, and flights for admitted students regardless of nationality. American University of Sharjah (AUS) is the UAE's top-ranked university and offers strong STEM and business programs.

Saudi Arabia

KAUST Offers Fully Funded Graduate Programs in STEM

King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST) in Saudi Arabia offers fully funded MS and PhD programs in engineering, computer science, environmental science, and more. All instruction is in English. Strong GRE scores and research experience are essential for admission.

Qatar

Education City Qatar — Home to Carnegie Mellon, Georgetown & More

Qatar Foundation's Education City hosts branch campuses of Carnegie Mellon, Georgetown, Northwestern, Cornell, and other top US universities. Students can earn degrees from these institutions while based in Doha, with scholarships widely available to international students.

South Korea

Seoul National University & KAIST — Fully Funded GKS Scholarships

The Korean Government Scholarship Program (GKS/KGSP) offers full funding for undergraduate and graduate students at top Korean universities including Seoul National University, KAIST, and POSTECH. Apply through Korean embassies by March each year for September admission.

China

Chinese Government Scholarship — Full Funding at Peking, Tsinghua & More

The Chinese Government Scholarship (CSC) offers full tuition, accommodation, and stipend for international students at Chinese universities including Peking University, Tsinghua, and Fudan. Applications open December–April each year for September enrollment. Strong academic records required.

Ireland

Trinity College Dublin & UCD — Gateway to EU After Brexit

Ireland's top universities — Trinity College Dublin, University College Dublin, and UCC — are increasingly attractive post-Brexit as they offer EU access with English-language instruction. Applications via CAO for undergraduate and directly to universities for graduate programs. September intake is primary.
