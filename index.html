About Tutoring Jedi

Welcome to Tutoring Jedi, where we believe every student has the potential to become a master of their own learning journey. Like the legendary Jedi, we understand that true mastery comes from dedication, personalized guidance, and the right mentorship.

Based in Toronto, Ontario, Canada, Tutoring Jedi specializes in standardized test prep (SAT, ACT, GRE, GMAT), university entrance counselling, high school Math and Science and university-level courses in Statistics and Math — taught by Neil Chakraborty, an expert tutor with 15+ years of experience teaching students across the world. Serving students across the Greater Toronto Area and online worldwide

At Tutoring Jedi, we don't just teach subjects — we build the analytical thinking, exam strategy, and academic confidence that produce lasting results. Every student receives a personalized roadmap aligned to their goals, timeline, and learning style.

Whether you're looking to get into top universities around the world, or need help in your high school or university level courses, Tutoring Jedi provides the expert guidance and support you need to succeed.
