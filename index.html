Teaching Philosophy

Neil believes that effective teaching starts with understanding each student as an individual. Instruction is tailored to strengths, challenges, and learning styles.

The focus is on conceptual clarity, critical thinking, and confidence-building for long-term success.

Neil has over 15 years of experience tutoring Math, Science, and preparing students for SAT, ACT, GMAT, and GRE exams.

He works at the University of Toronto and is an adjunct professor at Seneca Polytechnic with extensive experience in analytics and education.
