About Tutoring Jedi

Welcome to Tutoring Jedi, where we believe every student has the potential to become a master of their own learning journey. Like the legendary Jedi, we understand that true mastery comes from dedication, personalized guidance, and the right mentorship.

At Tutoring Jedi, we don't just teach subjects – we empower students to develop critical thinking skills, build confidence, and discover their unique learning styles. Our approach combines proven educational techniques with innovative strategies tailored to each student's individual needs and goals.

Whether you're struggling with challenging concepts, preparing for important exams, or looking to excel beyond your current level, Tutoring Jedi provides the expert guidance and support you need to succeed.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To create a world where every student has access to personalized, high-quality tutoring that unlocks their full academic potential and inspires a lifelong love of learning. We envision education as an empowering journey where challenges become opportunities and every student discovers the confidence to master any subject.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional one-on-one tutoring that adapts to each student's unique learning style, pace, and goals. We are committed to building strong foundations, fostering critical thinking skills, and nurturing academic confidence through patient guidance, proven methodologies, and genuine care for each student's success.

Teaching Philosophy & Tutor

Teaching Philosophy

Neil brings years of dedicated experience in personalized education, with a passion for helping students unlock their full potential. His teaching philosophy centers on understanding each student as an individual, identifying their unique strengths and challenges, and crafting customized learning strategies that make complex concepts accessible and engaging.


With expertise across multiple subjects and grade levels, Neil has helped countless students not only improve their grades but also develop the critical thinking skills and confidence needed for long-term academic success. His patient, supportive approach creates a comfortable learning environment where students feel encouraged to ask questions, make mistakes, and grow.


Neil believes that every student can excel when given the right tools, techniques, and encouragement. His commitment to excellence and genuine investment in each student's journey has made him a trusted mentor for families throughout the community

The focus is on conceptual clarity, critical thinking, and confidence-building for long-term success.

Neil Chakraborty

Founder & Lead Tutor

Neil Chakraborty has 15 years+ tutoring experience in high school Math and Science courses and counselling for competitive exams like SAT, ACT, GMAT & GRE. He is passionate in teaching students and helping them succeed beyond their potential. He consistently guided students to SAT score improvements of 100–150 points and ACT improvements of 5-10 points, helping them achieve results that supported successful admissions to Ivy League and top schools across the globe. His students have received acceptances to Harvard, Stanford, UPenn, Yale, UChicago, USC, Rice, UC Berkeley, John Hopkins, Northwestern, Universty of Michigan, Northeastern, Tufts, UCs, BU, UofT, UBC and McGill. He also co-founded an admission counselling consultancy and successfully helped 100s of individuals gain admission to the best business schools across the world.

In his day job, he is an institutional research and planning analyst working at the University of Toronto, top ranked university in Canada. He also is an Adjunct Professor at Seneca Polytechnic and teaches a variety of courses including Business Analytics and Research Methods. He has spent more than two decades working in research, project management, data analytics spanning multiple countries including US and Canada. He has a double Masters in Business Administration from University of Utah, and earned certification in Project management and SAS. He has also undergraduate and master's degrees majoring in Physics and Statistics.

He is also active outside of work and is a Board of Director at multiple non-profits in Canada. Among his other interests, he enjoys running marathons, swimming and hiking.
