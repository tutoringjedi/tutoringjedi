Why Staying Informed Matters

Admissions landscapes change every year. Here's why keeping up with the latest news gives you a real advantage.

📅

Deadlines Change

Early consideration deadlines, test requirements, and application windows shift every cycle. Missing a single date can cost you a full year.

📝

Test Policies Are Shifting

More schools are reinstating SAT, ACT, GMAT, and GRE requirements after years of test-optional policies. Knowing which schools require scores gives you time to prepare.

🎓

Scholarship Deadlines Come First

Many scholarship deadlines precede admission deadlines by 6–12 months. You can't apply for funding you didn't know about.

🌍

Global Options Are Growing

Top universities in Singapore, Germany, Japan, and the Middle East offer world-class education, often at a fraction of US or UK costs — but most students never consider them.
