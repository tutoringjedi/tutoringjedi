United Kingdom

Key UCAS deadlines, university news, and trends for students applying to UK universities.

UCAS Deadlines

Key Date

UCAS Equal Consideration Deadline: January 14, 2026

The UCAS equal consideration deadline for 2026 entry is January 14, 2026. Applying before this date ensures all universities consider your application equally. Applications submitted after this date are considered at universities' discretion.

Oxford & Cambridge

Oxbridge & Medicine: October 15, 2025 Early Deadline — Record Applications

41,010 UK 18-year-olds applied by the October 15 deadline — up 5.3% year over year. Medicine applications alone rose 10.4%. Cambridge interviews ran December 1–19, 2025 with results in January 2026.

Record Numbers

Record 323,360 UK 18-Year-Olds Applied by January 2025 Deadline

UCAS data shows total applicants reached 600,660 — up 1% year over year. International applicants rose 2.7%, with the largest increases from China, Ireland, and the USA. Global confidence in UK higher education remains strong.

UK Trends & Policy

STEM Surge

Engineering Up 13%, Mathematical Sciences Up 7% in UCAS Applications

Demand for STEM courses is growing fastest at higher-tariff UK universities. Engineering and technology applications rose 13% and mathematical sciences 7% in the 2025 cycle. This is creating more competition in already selective programs.

Access

UCAS Removes Application Fee for Students on Free School Meals

From 2025 entry onwards, students receiving free school meals are exempt from the £28.50 UCAS application fee. Teachers verify eligibility through the adviser portal. This makes UK higher education more accessible for students from lower-income backgrounds.

2025

Read more

Clearing

28,000+ Courses Available Through UCAS Clearing in 2025

UCAS Clearing — which opens after A-level results in August — had over 28,000 courses available in 2025. Total acceptances were up 3% on the same point last year, with UK 18-year-old acceptances showing a 4% increase. Clearing remains a strong route for many students.

TU Munich & LMU Munich Remain Tuition-Free for All Students

Germany's top universities — TU Munich, LMU Munich, Heidelberg, and Humboldt Berlin — remain tuition-free for both domestic and international students. Most programs require strong German proficiency. English-taught master's programs are widely available for September intake.

Scholarships & Funding

Erasmus Mundus

Erasmus Mundus Joint Master Degrees — Full Funding for International Students

Erasmus Mundus scholarships cover full tuition plus a monthly living allowance for study across multiple European universities. Highly competitive — apply 12+ months in advance. Programs span STEM, social sciences, humanities, and more across 40+ countries.

DAAD Germany

DAAD Scholarships Fund Study & Research in Germany for Internationals

The German Academic Exchange Service (DAAD) offers scholarships for study and research stays at German universities. Programs available at undergraduate, master's, and doctoral levels. Apply 6–12 months before your intended start date.
